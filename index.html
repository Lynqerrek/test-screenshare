Share your screen to a Cardboard VR viewer
PEER-TO-PEER · NO SERVER · LAN / INTERNET
▸ How It Works
This uses WebRTC peer-to-peer to stream your screen directly to your phone's browser. Your phone opens mobile-vr.html and splits the stream into two eyes for Cardboard VR. Both devices need to be on the same Wi-Fi or use the public signaling option.
1
Click Start Screen Share and select your window or screen. Chrome/Edge recommended. Firefox works too.
2
Copy the connection code or scan the QR with your phone. Open mobile-vr.html on your phone, paste the code.
3
Put your phone in the Cardboard headset and enjoy. Tap the screen on mobile to go fullscreen.
